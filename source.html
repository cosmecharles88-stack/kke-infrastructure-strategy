The High Cost of Inaction: Liability & Risk

Deferring maintenance is not a cost-saving measure; it is a massive transfer of budget into high-risk legal and safety liabilities.

⚖️ CRITICAL RISK

Trip Hazard Liability

Any vertical displacement over **1/4 inch** is a primary target for "slip and fall" litigation. Settlements for sidewalk injuries in Texas often exceed **$50,000 to $100,000** per incident, plus legal defense fees.

Inaction Cost:

4x - 10x Repair Cost

♿ FEDERAL MANDATE

ADA Non-Compliance

Title II of the ADA requires municipalities to maintain accessible paths. Failure can result in Department of Justice (DOJ) enforcement, federal fines, and mandatory, expensive "Transition Plan" implementations under court supervision.

Legal Exposure:

Uncapped Fines & Mandates

🌊 ASSET DECAY

Ponding & Subgrade Failure

Standing water (ponding) accelerates asphalt stripping and subgrade saturation. In winter, ice patches create immediate traffic safety hazards and increase insurance claims for vehicle damage and personal injury.

Asset Life Reduction:

50% Shorter Lifespan
